## Assembly Sequence (Standard Tier)

1. **Mark window position** on front face of existing hive box (180×100 mm centred, 80 mm from bottom)
2. **Route recess** 12 mm deep using router or chisel — do not penetrate inner wall
3. **Test-fit acrylic panel** — verify ≥ 6.35 mm bee space from inner face
4. **Apply silicone bead** around outer perimeter of recess (outer face only)
5. **Set acrylic panel** into recess; press and cure 24 hours
6. **Fit MDF shutter channels** above and below window — secure with M4 brass screws
7. **Slide in PVC shutter** — test full travel, verify zero light leak when closed
8. **Insert brass stop pin** at top of channel
9. **Apply beeswax** to shutter side edges for smooth operation
10. **Fit feeder trough bracket** at hive entrance — level check critical
11. **Set HDPE trough** in bracket, insert coir float strip
12. **Fill 1L PET bottle** with 1:1 sugar syrup, invert over trough lid, set in place
13. **Wrap XPS insulation** on sides and rear — secure with self-adhesive foil tape
14. **Fit mesh ventilation strip** on rear top edge — staple into position
15. **Final check:** shutter test, feeder flow test, bee space verification, ventilation airflow test
