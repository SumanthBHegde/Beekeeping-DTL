# 02 - Dot Voting

**Purpose:**
This folder acts as the first filtration mechanism in our Ideation pipeline. 

**Contents:**
- `voting-results.md`: The documented winners of the dot-voting exercise.

**Methodology:**
After generating dozens of raw ideas in the Brainstorming phase, it is impossible to rigorously test every single one. The team uses "Dot-Voting" (where each member gets a limited number of 'dots' or votes to place on their favorite ideas) to rapidly surface the concepts that have the highest perceived value, feasibility, and alignment with the user personas. Only the top-voted ideas survive to reach the Concept Notes stage.
