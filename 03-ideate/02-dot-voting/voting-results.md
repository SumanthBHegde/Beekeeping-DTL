# Dot-Voting Results

After reviewing the comprehensive list of ideas generated for our HMW questions in the [idea-log.md](../01-brainstorming/idea-log.md), the team participated in a dot-voting exercise to quickly identify the most promising concepts to take to the formal selection matrix.

*(Note: Each team member is given 5 'dots' to place on their favorite ideas across any category).*

## Top Voted Ideas (Moving Forward to Matrix)

These are the final selected ideas that emerged victorious from the dot-voting session. Their rationale is directly tied to the primary needs identified in our [Pain Point Analysis](../../02-define/02-pain-point-analysis.md) and [Scope Decision](../../02-define/06-scope-decision.md).

1. **Acrylic transparent window on the side for visual inspection without opening with a sliding cover to block light when not inspecting**
   - **Votes:** [X] dots
   - *Why it stood out:* Combines inspection safety with light control, directly addressing the beginner fear of stings and colony temperature disruption noted in the [Empathy Insights](../../01-empathy/08-insights/README.md).

2. **External sugar syrup feeder with refill funnel outside, no lid opening needed**
   - **Votes:** [X] dots
   - *Why it stood out:* Allows for safe, non-disruptive feeding during monsoon/winter without heat loss or stings. Resolves the heat-loss pain point from our [Need Statements](../../02-define/03-need-statements.md).

3. **Modular super with clip connectors to attach to any existing Indian box**
   - **Votes:** [X] dots
   - *Why it stood out:* Hits the strict < ₹4,000 cost constraint defined in the [Scope Decision](../../02-define/06-scope-decision.md) by allowing farmers to reuse their existing boxes instead of buying entirely new systems.

4. **Auto harvesting flow hive design — twist mechanism per frame and Single output pipe connecting all frames — one-tap honey harvest Gravity-tilt harvest — tilt hive slightly so honey flows without pumping**
   - **Votes:** [X] dots
   - *Why it stood out:* An ambitious combination of ideas to completely eliminate the most disruptive and painful part of beekeeping: the harvest. Addresses the FPO / Society Leader desire for easier value extraction from [Personas](../../01-empathy/05-personas/README.md).

5. **Donut-shaped water/oil container on the standing pole to block ant access (Doubles as a water source for the bees)**
   - **Votes:** [X] dots
   - *Why it stood out:* A brilliant multi-functional mechanical defense against the most common pest (ants) highlighted in the [Secondary Research](../../01-empathy/01-research/secondary-research.md), while providing necessary hydration for the bees.

6. **Thermal insulation layer from inside the hive box walls**
   - **Votes:** [X] dots
   - *Why it stood out:* Passive, low-effort solution to maintain the delicate internal temperature of the colony, solving the severe heat-loss issue documented in the [Problem Statement](../../02-define/04-problem-statement.md).
