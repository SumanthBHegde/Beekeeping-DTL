# Ideate Phase

Welcome to the **Ideation Phase** of the Efficient Beekeeping and Hive Box project. 

The goal of this phase is to transition from the deep understanding developed in the `01-empathy` and `02-define` phases into tangible, actionable solutions. Rather than jumping straight to the first obvious idea, we employed a highly structured funnel to ensure our final chosen direction is both creative and rigorously validated against our strict project constraints.

## Folder Structure (The Ideation Pipeline)

Our ideation workflow follows a strict chronological pipeline, documented in the following sub-directories:

1. **[01-brainstorming](01-brainstorming/)**: The generation of a wide volume of raw ideas using specific creative methodologies (Brainwriting 6-3-5, SCAMPER, Reverse Brainstorming) mapped to our core "How Might We" questions.
2. **[02-dot-voting](02-dot-voting/)**: The initial, rapid team-filtering exercise to identify the most promising concepts from the raw brainstorming log.
3. **[03-concept-notes](03-concept-notes/)**: Detailed, fleshed-out descriptions of the top-voted ideas, including user workflows, material requirements, and modularity.
4. **[04-selection-matrix](04-selection-matrix/)**: The final, rigorous scoring of the top concepts against our core constraints (Cost < ₹4,000, Safety, Non-Disruptive, Feasibility).

The output of this phase—detailed in the selection matrix—is the final approved direction that we will build in the `04-prototype` phase.
