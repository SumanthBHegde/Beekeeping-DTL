# Quotes

The strongest quotes captured during our user interviews, grouped by core themes.

## The Fear & Beginner Struggle
> *"My biggest fear is getting stung. If I open the box in the afternoon, they will attack."*  
> — **Sushanth Hegde (Enthusiastic Beginner)**

> *"As a beginner, you don't know when the bees are sick until it's too late. I lost 3 colonies because of this."*  
> — **Akshay Kedla (1-Year Experience)**

> *"Fear and lack of maintenance knowledge are why beginners quit. When a colony flies away due to weather, they get demotivated."*  
> — **Harsha Hegde (FPO CEO)**

## Climate & Operational Friction
> *"Opening the box in the rainy season ruins the hive's temperature for two days. We need a way to feed them from the outside."*  
> — **Dhruv Bhat (Commercial Beekeeper)**

> *"Heat is the biggest problem. If the sun hits the box directly, they won't come up to the 'super' to store honey."*  
> — **Ullas Naik (Local Beekeeper)**

> *"If you don't maintain the exact 7mm bee space, they glue everything together. It’s a mess to pull the frames out."*  
> — **Akshay Kedla (1-Year Experience)**

## The "Gadget" Reality vs. Economics
> *"Automated boxes are 25k. We only get 10kg of honey a year. The math doesn't work for Indian farmers."*  
> — **Ullas Naik (Local Beekeeper)**

> *"If you design something new, make it modular. It should fit on top of our existing boxes so we don't have to throw them away."*  
> — **Dhruv Bhat (Commercial Beekeeper)**

> *"A transparent window won't work. The bees will just cover the glass with wax from the inside because they want darkness."*  
> — **Harsha Hegde (FPO CEO)**

## Untapped Potential
> *"Everyone is focusing only on honey. No one in India is extracting Royal Jelly or Bee Venom commercially. We need technology for that."*  
> — **Harsha Hegde (FPO CEO)**
